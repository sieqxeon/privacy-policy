App Information, Privacy Policy & Support

App Information

haguren

haguren is a group location-sharing app designed for touring and driving groups. Members can share their real-time location with a group, propose and vote on destinations, coordinate schedules, and get notified when a group member is nearby.

  • Features
    • Real-time location sharing within a group (on/off controlled by the user)
    • Destination proposals with comments and likes
    • Route search (toll roads on/off comparison)
    • Schedule coordination within a group
    • Proximity notifications (Premium)
    • Support for up to 3 groups created and 10 groups joined per user

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information haguren collects, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in, we use Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In, Sign in with Apple, or Anonymous sign-in). We store a unique user ID (UID) associated with your account. We do not receive or store your Google/Apple account password.
  • Profile information you provide: Display name, icon, and color you choose when joining or creating a group.
  • Location data: When you turn on location sharing, your device's precise location is sent to Cloud Firestore and shown to other members of the same group in real time. Location sharing is off by default and can be turned off at any time. When no one else in the group is sharing, your location is not transmitted even if sharing is turned on.
  • Group data: Group name and group password (used to control who can join a group), destination proposals, comments, votes, and schedule responses you submit.
  • Advertising identifiers: This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS, subject to your App Tracking Transparency choice, or the Android Advertising ID on Android) to provide and measure ads. On iOS, we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain consent where required (e.g., EEA/UK users) before ads are personalized.
  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to haguren Pro, purchase transactions are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. We receive your subscription status (active/inactive) to unlock Premium features, but we do not receive your payment card details.

How Information Is Used

Location and group data are used solely to provide the core functionality of the app: showing group members' locations, destinations, and schedules to other members of the same group. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore) - Google LLC
  • Google AdMob (advertising) - Google LLC
  • Google Maps / Google Places API - Google LLC
  • Mapbox Directions API (route search) - Mapbox Inc.
  • RevenueCat (subscription management) - RevenueCat, Inc.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play (subscription billing)

These third parties may collect information used to identify you as described in their own privacy policies. The information collected by these third parties is used for providing and improving their services.

Subscriptions

haguren Pro is an auto-renewing monthly subscription that unlocks Premium features (such as proximity notifications). Payment is charged to your Apple ID or Google Play account at confirmation of purchase, and the subscription automatically renews unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings. Purchases are subject to the refund policies of the Apple App Store or Google Play, as applicable.

Data Retention

Group membership records, proposals, and schedule data are retained until you leave the group, the group is disbanded by its leader, or you delete your account data by contacting us. Location data sent while sharing is active is used to display real-time positions to group members and is not retained for purposes beyond providing this feature.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • Location sharing can be turned on or off at any time within the app.
  • You can leave a group at any time from the group list on the login screen.
  • You can request deletion of your account data by contacting us at the email address below.
